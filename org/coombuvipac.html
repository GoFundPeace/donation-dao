Education, Land and Reincorporation

In 2016 our community decided to work towards peace building and its maintenance. This decision took us to transform our territory and to build our dreams. The popular library Alfonso Cano is part of this collective dream. The library was inaugurated in 2017 as a community exercise to improve the access to education and knowledge production. We aimed to have a space for collective thinking about the territory dynamics and to propose solutions to the community needs.

Since the signature of the peace agreement in 2016, our community has been growing and several children have been born in the territory. Currently, the community is formed by around 300 people. Every week, we receive guests from other villages and people who come to know our significant experience of reincorporation in Colombia. Since then, we have felt the need to expand our library, which only has a space of 55m2. With a growing children and youth community, we need adequate spaces for their integral development.
